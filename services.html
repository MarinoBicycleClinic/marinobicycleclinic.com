What I can do for you

Marino Bicycle Clinic believes in true professionalism, which is why I am available whenever you need me. Whatever you need fixed, I repair technician will happily advise and assist you. I am available to answer all of your questions and concerns. Not 100% satisfied with your repair? I will do everything I can to correct the problem. I want you to be a satisfied customer and am confident you’ll come back for more.

Basic Bicycle Tune-up - $45.00

This includes cleaning grease off the gears & chain, adjusting brakes & derailleurs, lube cables & pivot points as needed, truing of the wheels, tightening all bolts & hardware, a quick wipe down, fill the air in tires, test ride & a safety check.

*Deluxe tune-up - $80.00

Includes all adjustments in the Complete Tune-Up plus:

  • Full drive train clean (parts are removed and cleaned in parts washer)
  • Full bike clean (frameset, components, and wheels cleaned and polished to remove all dirt and grime)
* Deluxe Tune-Ups will have a $20 miscellaneous parts estimate to include any small parts that may be needed such as cables, cable housing, or tubes. This is in place so that I don't have to call you if I come upon something minor. Any amount not used will be removed from the order.

*Complete Overhaul - $190.00

A complete overhaul includes all of the above Tune-Up adjustments. The bike is completely taken apart, cleaned and the bearings are repacked (parts and bearings are extra)

* The overhaul tune-up will have a $20 miscellaneous parts estimate to include any small parts that may be needed such as bearings, cables, cable housing, or tubes. This is in place so that I don't have to call you if I come upon something minor. Any amount not used will be removed from the order.
